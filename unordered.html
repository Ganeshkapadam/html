Social media Apps

WhatsApp

  • Video calls let users communicate using video, as well as voice and text.
  • Build community. Group conversations made simple.

Instagram

  • Check out Stories and live videos from your favorite people.
  • Send messages, photos and videos to a friend or select group of people.
  • Browse the latest trends from your favorite brands and creators.

Youtube

  • Audio/video file upload.
  • Live Captioning.
  • Reporting/Analytics.
  • Social Sharing.
  • YouTube is a social platform that allows for two-way communication between brands and their audience.
