Pawan Kalyan's Political Career

Pawan Kalyan, a prominent figure in Telugu cinema, ventured into politics in the early 2010s.

He founded the Jana Sena Party in 2014 with the aim of bringing about political change in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

Known for his charismatic persona and mass appeal, Pawan Kalyan campaigned actively, advocating for various social and political issues.

Over the years, he has influenced state politics through his speeches, rallies, and engagement with the public.

His journey in politics continues to evolve, making him a significant figure in South Indian politics.
